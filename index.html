Welcome to ICG2E 2023
INTERNATIONAL CONFERENCE

The 1th International Conference on Geoscience and Engineering Geology (ICG2E) is the first international seminar held by the Department of Geological Engineering, Faculty of Engineering, Hasanuddin University. This seminar is planned to be held routinely every year. In this activity, apart from providing opportunities for researchers to present and publish their research results, this seminar also emphasizes increasing and strengthening triple helix cooperation among three elements (university, industry, government or UIG) in improving the quality of research results in order to support National development.

Submission

Submission Template

Submission Instructions (Presenter) :

  1. Please log in to the system and fill in all the mandatory fields.
  2. Upon a successful login, you will see the main interface. Click on "New Submission".
  3. Follow the instructions, step by step, to fill the online form (add Authors, Title of your paper, list of Keywords to your submission manually).
  4. Submit the abstract/full paper. Upload an abstract/fulpaper of upload field. Once your online submission is successful, you will receive an e-mail confirming receipt of your submission and your submission will be given a reference number. If you have not received the acknowledgement email or the result notification emails within 2 (two) days after submission, you can contact us by email at icosfmipauh@gmail.com.
  5. Use the following template for the Abstract Template.
Abstract Template

Full Paper Submission Rules:

  • Full paper must be submitted before April 27th, 2022- 23:59 (All times and date are in GMT +8).
  • Full paper can be submitted at easychair by updating the abstract that has been uploaded.
  • Selected paper will be offered to be published by Scopus.
  • All full paper must be prepared according to the guidelines provided in the AIPC Template.

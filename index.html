Welcome to ICG2E 2023
INTERNATIONAL CONFERENCE

The 1th International Conference on Geoscience and Engineering Geology (ICG2E) is the first international seminar held by the Department of Geological Engineering, Faculty of Engineering, Hasanuddin University. This seminar is planned to be held routinely every year. In this activity, apart from providing opportunities for researchers to present and publish their research results, this seminar also emphasizes increasing and strengthening triple helix cooperation among three elements (university, industry, government or UIG) in improving the quality of research results in order to support National development.
